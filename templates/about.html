About Basketball Shot Analysis

How It Works

Our basketball shot analysis service uses cutting-edge computer vision and biomechanical assessment to provide professional-grade feedback on your shooting form.

Technology Stack
  • MediaPipe: Google's pose estimation framework for real-time body landmark detection
  • OpenCV: Video processing and computer vision operations
  • Biomechanical Analysis: Advanced joint angle calculations and movement assessment
  • Machine Learning: Intelligent phase detection and feedback generation
Analysis Features
Joint Angle Analysis
  • Elbow extension measurement
  • Knee bend assessment
  • Wrist snap analysis
  • Hip and shoulder alignment
Shot Phase Detection
  • Load/Dip phase identification
  • Ascent and power generation
  • Release point detection
  • Follow-through analysis
Coaching Methodology

Our analysis is based on proven basketball shooting fundamentals used by professional coaches and sports scientists. The system compares your form against ideal biomechanical ranges and provides specific, actionable feedback.

Ideal Shot Metrics
Elbow Extension:
160° - 180° at release
Knee Bend:
110° - 130° during load
Shot Arc:
45° - 50° optimal trajectory
Release Time:
0.8 - 1.2 seconds total
